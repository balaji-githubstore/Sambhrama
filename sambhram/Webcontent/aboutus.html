Who We Are

Sambhrama Charitable trust is the brainchild of a group of enthusiastic Doctors and Engineers. Every charity begins with a heart that is kind and a cause which is worthy. Our team found a common cause that is not going to change the world at once but will do in due course which is to facilitate the needy. Having this Collaboration through the Sambharama Charitable Trust was a dream come true to our team with a gritty hope that first step towards a steady goal had been set in motion. Every dream shall remain a dream if we don't work towards its achievement. Our programmes are diversified with a collective aim to help all those in need and spread joy to all those who we can.  

OUR TEAM

Ms R.Bhavya M.Tech-President

" Helping hands is better than praying lips"." I never imagined that visiting an orphanage would be such a life changing experience. My transformation story from praying lips to helping hands began with that experience. Quite literally in an orphanage, when a child about 3 ft tall glared his sight right through to my eyes, his meek expression seeking something I didn't understand at the time. I believe as a collective society we have survived on a simple strategy which is " Survival instincts" this survival instinct has kept us striving through generations of evolution. The society of today, requires the survived to help the less fortunate revive towards surviving both emotionally and physically. Being able to support these tots at orphanages, to enable them and to empower them has made me look forward to every dawn with a rising sense of contentment."
